Protein Yoghurt

Protein yoghurt is a quick source of protein and fat, perfect as a post-workout meal. It is quick to prepare and tastes great

Ingredients

  • Whey protein - any flavour 2-3 scoops
  • Unflavoured greek yoghurt 100-200g
  • Milk 2-4 tbsp
  • Toppings
    • blueberries
    • banana
    • dessicated coconut

Steps

  1. Add 2-3 scoops of whey protein to bowl
  2. Add your yoghurt on top, 100g for a thicker protein yoghurt, or up to 200g for a creamier yoghurt
  3. Adding milk alters the texture, upwards of 4 tbsp will thin the protein yoghurt significantly
  4. Mix the whey protein into the yoghurt, a spatula is best for this
  5. Once mixed, place bowl in freezer for up to 1 hour
  6. Lastly, add desired toppings and serve
