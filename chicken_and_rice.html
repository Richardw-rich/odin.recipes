Chicken and Rice

This is the perfect meal to prep ahead of time, this recipe will make 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken
  • 2 cups rice
  • Optional toppings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Start cooking rice first, whether by using a rice cooker or a pot use three cups of water for two cups of rice
  2. Preheat pan before cutting up chicken
  3. Cut up chicken into approximately 2cm cubes
  4. Add chicken to pan, ideally without the use of cooking oils
  5. Mix chicken every few minutes as it starts to cook and turn brown on the surface
  6. Remember to check chicken is cooked through by cutting the largest piece to inspect its centre
  7. Portion out both the chicken and rice into 4 equal portions
  8. Add desired toppings such as steamed broccoli, salt/pepper
