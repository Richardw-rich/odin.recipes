Microwave Omlette

Eggs and the optional cheese are both high in protein and rich in healty fats. Making this will take less than 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Eggs, between 3 and 5
  • Milk, approx 1 tbsp per egg
  • Cheese 50-80g
  • Toppings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • other proteins such as ham, chicken or mince

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl
  2. Add milk
  3. Whisk eggs and milk with fork until fully combined
  4. place bowl in microwave for 1.30 minutes
  5. whisk eggs again once finished in microwave
  6. While eggs are in microwave a second time, cut desired amount of cheese into thin slices
  7. microwave a third time to melt cheese
  8. If required, mix loosely and microwave a fourth time
  9. Add salt/pepper and any other toppings and serve
